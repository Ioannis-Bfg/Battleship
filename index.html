RULES

  • The game is played on two grids, one for each player.
  • Each player has a fleet of ships to place on their grid.
  • Players take turns calling out a grid reference to attack their opponent's ships.
  • If a ship is hit, it is marked on the grid.
  • The game continues until all of one player's ships are sunk.
